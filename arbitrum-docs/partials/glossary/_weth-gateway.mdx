[Token Gateway](/intro/glossary#token-gateway) for handing the bridging of wrapped Ether (`WETH`). `WETH` is unwrapped on the parent chain and rewrapped on the parent chain upon depositing (and vice-versa upon withdrawing), ensuring `WETH` on the child chain always remains collateralized.
