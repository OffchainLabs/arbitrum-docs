A blockchain that runs on the Arbitrum protocol. Arbitrum chains are EVM compatible, and use an underlying EVM chain (e.g., Ethereum) for settlement and for succinct fraud-proofs (as needed). Arbitrum chains come in two forms: [Arbitrum Rollup Chains](/intro/glossary#arbitrum-rollup-chain) and [Arbitrum AnyTrust Chains](/intro/glossary#arbitrum-anytrust-chain).
