In the [BoLD](/intro/glossary#bold) [Challenge Protocol](/intro/glossary#challenge-protocol), two [edges](/intro/glossary#edge) are _rivals_ when they share a starting point but make incompatible claims about the same range of chain history. Rivaling an edge stops its unrivaled timer and unlocks [bisection](/intro/glossary#bisection) moves to resolve the disagreement.
