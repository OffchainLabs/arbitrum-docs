---
title: Custom gas token
key: custom-gas-token
titleforSort: Custom gas token
---

A non-ETH ERC-20 token chosen at [Arbitrum chain](/intro/glossary#arbitrum-chain) deployment time to be used for paying transaction fees on that chain. Deposits work by escrowing the ERC-20 in the chain's bridge on the [parent chain](/intro/glossary#parent-chain) and crediting the equivalent amount as the native asset on the child chain, where it replaces ETH everywhere ETH would otherwise be charged for gas.
