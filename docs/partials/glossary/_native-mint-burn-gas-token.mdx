---

A [custom gas token](/intro/glossary#custom-gas-token) variant whose supply on the [Arbitrum chain](/intro/glossary#arbitrum-chain) is controlled by mint and burn calls to the `ArbNativeTokenManager` precompile (address `0x73`) rather than by bridging from the parent chain. Only accounts in the chain's `nativeTokenOwners` set — managed through the `ArbOwner` precompile — may mint or burn. Suited to closed-economy chains that need full control over gas-token issuance.
