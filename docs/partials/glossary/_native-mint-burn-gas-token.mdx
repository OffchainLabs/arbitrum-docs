title: Native mint/burn gas token
key: native-mint-burn-gas-token
titleforSort: Native mint/burn gas token
---

A [custom gas token](/intro/glossary#custom-gas-token) variant whose supply on the [Arbitrum chain](/intro/glossary#arbitrum-chain) is controlled by mint/burn calls from a [chain-owner](/intro/glossary#chain-owner)-authorized account, rather than bridged in from the parent chain. Suited to closed-economy chains that need full control over gas-token issuance.
