title: Proposer
key: proposer
titleforSort: Proposer
---

A [Validator](/intro/glossary#validator) running in MakeNodes mode under [BoLD](/intro/glossary#bold), actively submitting new [Assertions](/intro/glossary#assertion) to the parent chain. Proposers are bonded at the largest tier to deter delay attacks. Contrast with a [Watchtower Validator](/intro/glossary#watchtower-validator) (passive, no on-chain action) and a [Defensive Validator](/intro/glossary#defensive-validator) (acts only to counter an invalid claim).
