A primitive in the [BoLD](/intro/glossary#bold) [Challenge Protocol](/intro/glossary#challenge-protocol). An edge is a claim about a portion of an Arbitrum chain's history, identified by a starting and ending [history commitment](/intro/glossary#history-commitment). Validators bisect edges to narrow disagreement down to a single execution step.
