titleforSort: History commitment

A Merkle-tree root committing to a sequence of execution states—either block hashes or instruction-level state hashes, depending on the level of the dispute. Each [BoLD](/intro/glossary#bold) [edge](/intro/glossary#edge) carries a start and end history commitment to represent a claim over a range of chain history.
