The onchain governance body that owns and operates [Arbitrum One](/intro/glossary#arbitrum-one) and [Arbitrum Nova](/intro/glossary#arbitrum-nova). Holders of the **ARB** token vote on protocol upgrades, treasury actions, and [Arbitrum Expansion Program](/intro/glossary#arbitrum-expansion-program) revenue distributions.
