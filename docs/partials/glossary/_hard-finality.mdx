---
title: Hard finality
key: hard-finality
titleforSort: Hard finality
---

The state a transaction reaches once its [batch](/intro/glossary#batch) has been posted to the parent chain by the [Batch Poster](/intro/glossary#batch-poster) and that posting is itself final on the parent chain. Hard finality inherits the security of the parent chain and cannot be reverted without a parent-chain reorg. Contrast with [soft confirmation](/intro/glossary#soft-confirmation), which is immediate but trust-based on the honesty of the [Sequencer](/intro/glossary#sequencer).
