title: Challenge bond
key: challenge-bond
titleforSort: Challenge bond
---

The per-level [bond](/intro/glossary#bonder) required to open or move within a sub-challenge under [BoLD](/intro/glossary#bold). Each level of the dispute tree has its own configured bond amount, set at chain deployment. Challenge bonds are distinct from the larger assertion bond that backs the proposed [Assertion](/intro/glossary#assertion) itself.
