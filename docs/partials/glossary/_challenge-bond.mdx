---
title: Challenge bond
key: challenge-bond
titleforSort: Challenge bond
---

The smaller, per-level [bond](/intro/glossary#bonder) required to open or move within a sub-challenge under [BoLD](/intro/glossary#bold). Distinct from the larger assertion bond that backs the proposed [Assertion](/intro/glossary#assertion) itself, the challenge bond scales down at deeper levels of the dispute tree so that participation costs less as a dispute narrows.
