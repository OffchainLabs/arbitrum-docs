title: Upgrade Executor

titleforSort: Upgrade Executor
---

The single privileged contract — deployed once per [Arbitrum chain](/intro/glossary#arbitrum-chain) by the rollup creator — that authorizes protocol upgrades and admin actions. The Upgrade Executor owns both the chain's rollup contracts and its `ProxyAdmin`, so every privileged call to the core contracts is routed through it. Its admin role is typically held by the [chain owner](/intro/glossary#chain-owner).
