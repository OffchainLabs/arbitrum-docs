The privileged contract — deployed once per chain side — that authorizes protocol upgrades and admin actions on an [Arbitrum chain](/intro/glossary#arbitrum-chain). The Upgrade Executor sits at the root of every Arbitrum chain's permission hierarchy; only it can call upgrade entry points on the core contracts, and its admin role is typically held by the [chain owner](/intro/glossary#chain-owner).
