Arbitrum's variant of [WASM](/intro/glossary#wasm), used inside the [State Transition Function](/intro/glossary#state-transition-function) for [fraud-proof](/intro/glossary#fraud-proof) execution. WAVM is semantically equivalent to standard WASM but deterministic and provable onchain; the specific WAVM prover image in use is identified by the [WASM module root](/intro/glossary#wasm-module-root).
